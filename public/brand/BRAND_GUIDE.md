## WINNERS

### PRIMARY LOGO — **Image D**
> The full detailed lotus with two face silhouettes on cream background.
>
> **Why:** Embodies all five brand values simultaneously — the two faces represent *community and connection*, the lotus represents *healing and spiritual growth*, the warm gradient center represents *belonging and safety*, and "SOULCONNECT" in confident tracked caps communicates trust. This is the logo you print, pitch, and scale the brand on.

### APP ICON — **Image A**
> Dark purple rounded square with golden lotus + faces.
>
> **Why:** Already formatted for iOS/Android icon grids. The purple background provides strong contrast. The golden lotus is the icon mark that scales. Faces remain visible at 128px+. Consistent with brand palette.

### FAVICON — **Image C or E** (dark glowing lotus)
> The neon/glowing golden lotus on dark purple square.
>
> **Why:** At 16px × 16px, detail disappears. The glowing lotus silhouette on a dark background has the highest contrast and remains recognizable as a shape. The faces from Images A/B/D are invisible at favicon size — simplicity wins here.

### SOCIAL MEDIA PROFILE — **Image D**
> Full detailed lotus with two faces.
>
> **Why:** On Instagram/Facebook/LinkedIn profile grids, this crops as a circle at ~110px diameter. The centered lotus with both faces is immediately recognizable and distinct from generic wellness competitors. At 1080×1080 it is stunning.

### FOOTER LOGO — **Image B**
> Light-background outlined lotus with serif text.
>
> **Why:** The footer sits on a dark background — use the monochrome/white inversion of Image B. The outline-style lotus inverts cleanly to white without losing form. Clean, readable, not distracting from footer utility links.

### MONOCHROME VERSION — **Derived from Image B/D**
> Single-color (white or purple) version of the lotus outline.
>
> **Why:** Outline logos invert cleanly. Filled illustrations (C, E) lose dimensionality in monochrome. Image B's outlined style renders perfectly in white on dark, or purple on white.

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary — Display Headings
**Playfair Display** (Google Fonts)
- Weight: 700 / 800
- Use: H1, H2 section titles, logo wordmark style
- Feeling: timeless, elegant, editorial, healing

### Secondary — Body & UI
**Plus Jakarta Sans** → **Inter** (fallback)
- Weight: 400 / 500 / 600 / 700
- Use: All body copy, nav links, buttons, form labels
- Feeling: modern, clear, accessible

### Accent — Taglines
**Cormorant Garamond** (optional)
- Weight: 400 italic
- Use: Taglines, quotes, sub-headlines
- Feeling: poetic, soulful

## LOGO USAGE RULES

### ✅ DO
- Use Image D as the primary logo at 200px wide minimum
- Use Image A (app icon) at exact sizes: 512×512, 192×192, 128×128
- Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the "S" in SoulConnect around all edges
- Use white/monochrome version on dark backgrounds
- Use the full-color version on light/cream backgrounds

### ❌ DON'T
- Don't use Image D at sizes below 200px — detail is lost
- Don't stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo
- Don't place the primary logo on a busy photographic background without a color overlay
- Don't use the glow logos (C, E) as the primary identity — reserve for campaigns only
- Don't add drop shadows to the logo — the lotus already has internal depth
- Don't pair two logo variants in the same context (pick one)

## SPACING RULES

Clear space = **1× the lotus flower height** on all 4 sides.

For example, if the lotus is 80px tall, leave 80px clear space around the logo before any text, edge, or UI element.

Never crowd the logo. Breathing room = brand trust.

## FINAL DECISION REPORT

```
WINNER (Primary Logo):     Image D — Full lotus with two faces, SOULCONNECT caps
APP ICON:                  Image A — Purple square icon, golden lotus + faces
FAVICON:                   Image C/E — Glow lotus on dark (highest contrast at 16px)
SOCIAL PROFILE:            Image D — Most complete, stunning at 1080×1080
FOOTER LOGO:               Image B — Outline style inverts cleanly to white
MONOCHROME:                Derived from Image B — outline holds in single color
```

### Why These Choices Serve SoulConnect's Mission

**Heal** — The lotus is universally recognized as a symbol of healing from struggle.
Two people emerging from the same lotus = healing is shared, not solitary.

**Connect** — Two face silhouettes looking toward each other inside the lotus petals
is the most literal and beautiful expression of human connection in logo form.

**Grow** — The lotus rises from murky water toward light. Every time someone sees
this logo, they unconsciously receive the message: *growth is possible from difficulty*.

**Community** — The two faces make clear: this is not a solo meditation app.
This is a place where people find each other.

**Safety** — The enclosed, held quality of the lotus petals creates a sense of
containment and protection. The warm gold center = warmth, welcome, home.

**Belonging** — The faces inside the flower are not generic — they read as real
human silhouettes. Users will see themselves in this logo.

The selected logos collectively tell the SoulConnect story better than any tagline:
*You are not alone. We bloom together.*
